Regime Shift: EUR/USD Bullish Momentum Sustained by COT Positioning

OCT 24, 2024 EUR/USD BLUE
Bias: Long Volatility: Low Regime: Expansion

Executive Summary

The current market environment reflects a tactical stabilization in the Euro-complex. Our quantitative models indicate a transition from a neutral to a mild expansionary regime. This is primarily driven by a deceleration in USD momentum and a technical re-rating of Euro-zone inflation expectations.

COT Data Analysis

"Commitment of Traders (COT) report shows non-commercial long positions increasing by 12,400 contracts, while shorts have liquidated roughly 4,000. This net-long delta supports our bullish structural bias."

Technically, EUR/USD has established a firm base at the 1.0850 psychological level. We anticipate a continuation toward the 1.1020 liquidity pocket over the next 72 hours. Risk remains skewed toward a hawkish FOMC surprise, but current positioning suggests the 'path of least resistance' remains higher.

Target Zone

1.1020 — 1.1055

Stop Loss

1.0790

Investors should maintain a high-conviction long bias while monitoring the 10-year Treasury yield spread for any sudden decoupling. The regime remains constructive for pro-cyclical assets in the immediate term.
