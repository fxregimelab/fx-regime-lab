How I read regime

Four signal inputs. Each measures something the others cannot. Combined into a daily composite.

01

Rate differential

2Y and 10Y yield spreads between central banks. Wide and widening means directional pressure. Compressing reads as regime exhaustion.

02

COT positioning

CFTC Commitment of Traders, leveraged money and asset manager. 52-week percentile rank. Above the 85th or below the 15th flags crowding risk that can override the directional signal.

03

Implied volatility

30-day implied vol via ^EVZ (EUR/USD) and CME CVOL. High vol degrades trend signals. Low vol confirms carry regime. Acts as a gate on composite output.

04

Cross-asset

VIX, DXY correlation, gold correlation. Context layer: it does not drive the call but it modifies conviction. Broken correlations flag regime transitions before price moves.
