THE FRAMEWORK

Four signals. One regime call. Daily.

Each signal layer is sourced from public data, processed daily, and stored with timestamps. No black box. No manual override.

01

Rate Differentials

2Y and 10Y spreads sourced from FRED. Direction and momentum, not level. The carry thesis originates here.

02

COT Positioning

CFTC weekly reports converted to percentile rank. Crowded positioning above the 90th percentile is treated as reversal risk, not confirmation.

03

Realized Volatility

21-day rolling volatility normalized to 52-week range. Classified as LOW, NORMAL, HIGH, or EXTREME. Regime classification changes what every other signal means.

04

Cross-Asset Context

DXY, VIX, and oil read as USD regime inputs. Macro backdrop only, not equity analysis.
