Research terminal

Interactive regime depth for every pair

The terminal is the live workspace on top of the same daily dataset as the morning brief: rate differentials, CFTC positioning, implied volatility, correlations, and cross-asset context — organised by pair with expandable sections and charts. Use it when you want to drill past the headline narrative and inspect the signals directly.

What you can do

  • Pair desks — EUR/USD, USD/JPY, and USD/INR pages with regime, drivers, and invalidation-style context.
  • Live charts — ECharts-backed series aligned to the merged pipeline output (where data is available).
  • Home hub — snapshot cards, accuracy readouts, and quick jumps into full pair analysis.
  • Chart builder — experiment with series from the published data slice.

How it fits the site

The HTML brief and AI article view are narrative exports for a linear read. The terminal is for exploration and verification. Neither is investment advice or a solicitation — research and education only.
